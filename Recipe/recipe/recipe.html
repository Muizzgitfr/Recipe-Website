Chicken Tandoori Recipe

This is a simple recipe for chicken fajitas that serves 4 people. It's quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers, sliced
  • 4 small flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Beef Tacos Recipe

This is a simple recipe for beef tacos that serves 4 people. It's quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 8-10 taco shells
  • Shredded cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes for toppings
